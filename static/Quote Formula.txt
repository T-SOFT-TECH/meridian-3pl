Since your warehouse is **166 sqm** and you’re trying to start lean and win your first clients, I’d avoid enterprise-style pricing and use something simple that scales.

### Step 1 — Choose your charging model

For a small 3PL, charge in 5 layers:

1. **Account fee (base fee)**
2. **Storage**
3. **Inbound receiving**
4. **Pick & pack**
5. **Shipping + extras**

---

## A. Monthly Account Fee

Covers admin, software, support.

**Formula**

```text
Account Fee = Fixed Monthly Fee
```

Suggested:

* Starter → $299/month
* Growth → $499/month
* Custom → Quote

---

## B. Storage Pricing

Since you have **166 sqm**, don’t rent by sqm to clients initially.

Charge by **pallet position**.

Assumption:

* Usable warehouse ≈ 70%
* ~116 sqm usable
* Approx 40–55 pallet positions

**Formula**

```text
Storage Fee =
Pallet Positions × Monthly Rate
```

Suggested:

```text
1–10 pallets → $40/pallet
11–25 pallets → $35/pallet
26+ pallets → $30/pallet
```

Example:

```text
18 pallets × $35
=
$630/month
```

---

## C. Receiving (Inbound)

Charge for goods entering.

**Formula**

```text
Receiving =
Cartons Received × Rate
```

Suggested:

```text
1–100 cartons → $2/carton
101–300 → $1.50/carton
300+ → Custom
```

Example:

```text
150 cartons
=
$225
```

---

## D. Pick & Pack (Main Revenue Driver)

Suggested:

**Formula**

```text
PickPack =
Orders × Base Fee
+
Extra Items × Item Fee
```

Suggested:

```text
First item = $3.50
Additional item = $0.50
```

Example:

```text
200 orders
+
150 extra items

=
$775
```

---

## E. Shipping

Keep this simple initially.

**Formula**

```text
Shipping =
Courier Cost
+
10–15% Margin
```

Example:

```text
$10 freight
+
15%

=
$11.50 charged
```

---

## F. Extras

Optional:

```text
Returns = $7 each
Relabel = $1/unit
Kitting = $0.80/unit
Urgent dispatch = $50
Custom packaging = quote
```

---

### Fields for website form:

* Business name
* Average orders/month
* Average items/order
* Cartons received/month
* Pallets stored
* Average shipping postcode
* Extra services required

This structure should work well for a **166 sqm starter 3PL** and still look professional enough for your first 5–10 clients.
