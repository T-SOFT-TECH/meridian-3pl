## 1. About Us
### Who We Are
Meridian 3PL is a comprehensive third-party logistics provider dedicated to helping businesses streamline their supply chain operations through efficient warehousing, fulfillment, inventory management, and distribution solutions.

We understand that modern businesses need more than just storage space—they need a logistics partner capable of ensuring products move efficiently from warehouse shelves to customers' doorsteps. Our solutions are designed to reduce operational costs, improve order accuracy, increase delivery speed, and support business growth.

By combining industry expertise, technology-driven processes, and customer-focused service, Meridian 3PL enables businesses to focus on sales, marketing, and growth while we manage the complexities of logistics.

## 6. Our Logistics Process
1. **RECEIVE:** Products are received, inspected, and logged into inventory systems.
2. **STORE:** Inventory is securely organized and managed within our warehouse facilities.
3. **PROCESS:** Orders are received, picked, packed, and prepared for shipment.
4. **DELIVER:** Products are shipped and distributed through reliable transportation networks.
5. **REPORT:** Clients receive inventory and fulfillment insights for improved decision-making.
